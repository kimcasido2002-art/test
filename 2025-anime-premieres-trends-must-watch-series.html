Demographic data suggests viewers want more diverse characters. At the same time older fans might gravitate toward deeper, slower‑paced plots.

Influence of Global Anime Fandom

International viewers appear to push studios toward themes. Distribution channels might adapt, giving more subtitles and dubbed versions.

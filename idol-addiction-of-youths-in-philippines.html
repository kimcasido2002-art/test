Idol Addiction of Youths in Philippines: Understanding the Cultural Impact and Solutions

Maria Santos

Updated on 2024-06-01 | Posted on 2024-06-01

9 min read

idol addiction Philippinesyouth culture PhilippinesFilipino youth fandomidol fan culturehealthy fandom

Idol fandom in the Philippines seems to be growing fast, thanks to TikTok, K‑pop videos, and home‑grown singers. It might give teens a sense of belonging, yet could also steal time from school work. Parents could teach balanced fandom habits, encouraging discussion, limit scrolling, and promote real‑world friendships for healthier growth and emotional wellbeing.

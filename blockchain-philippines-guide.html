Blockchain Philippines: Capital, Events, and Outlook

Intro

  • Brief look at how blockchain grows in the Philippines, money flows, and events matter.

Blockchain Capital in the Philippines

  • Investment hubs cluster near Manila and Cebu, with venture groups pushing funds.
  • Key players may be startups, banks, or government units, all seeking returns.
  • Some angels hand seed money, but risk stays high.

Philippine Blockchain Week

  • This flagship week celebrates new ideas, teamwork, and progress in the scene.

  • History

    • It started a few years back and likely grew each edition.
    • Attendance rose from dozens to hundreds, showing interest.

  • Highlights

    • Workshops, hackathons, and mixers give chances to learn and network.

Blockchain and Bitcoin Conferences

  • Important conferences draw both particularly homegrown and foreign experts.

  • Top Events

    • Recurring gatherings focus on tech trends and feature speakers about regulation globally and development.
    • Topics cover security, smart contracts, policy, giving insight.

  • Impact

    • These meetings appear to significantly boost innovation, bring more investors, and push adoption.

Future Outlook

  • Emerging trends could include DeFi, NFTs, and more public‑private projects, therefore growth may keep rising.
  • Universities may work with firms, creating labs to train talent.
